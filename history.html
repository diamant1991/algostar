  • История

Foundation of the company "Elektrotechnische Spezialfabrik für Staubsauger und Gebläse" by Robert Schöttle in Stuttgart. 

Creating the "VENTUS", the company is one of the first vacuum-cleaner-manufacturers in Germany.

The company is renamed as "ELECTROSTAR" and develops the first hot-air hand-dryer worldwide. Already at that time 70 % of the products are exported into 49 countries.

The company ELECTROSTAR Schöttle GmbH & Co. moves, with inventory and the total of 30 employees th the today's location Reichenbach/Fils. 

Until 1945 the number of employees has increased to 150.

Der Starmix: die erste schnelllaufende Küchenmaschine kommt auf den Markt, ein echter Verkaufsschlager, der in der Wochenschau "Welt im Bild" präsentiert wird und zum Symbol des deutschen Wirtschaftswunders wird. 

Der Markenname "starmix" wird geboren.

The first multi-function vacuum cleaners and upright vacuum cleaners come into shops. Meanwhile the company employs already 650 employees. 

The foundation stone of the new building is placed.

1977 Robert Schöttle jun. enters the company in the 3rd generation. 

In the years between there were introduced several new developments in the hand dryer sector and the first industrial vacuum cleaner with filter cleaning is developed.

The enormous degree of fame of the starmix brand was going beyond the fame of the company brand "Electrostar". Back-to-back all products of Electrostar were getting the starmix signet consequently.

ELECTROSTAR baut das Exportgeschäft mit einem Joint Venture in Peking (China) weiter aus.

The Gorovoy family (Algo-Gruppe) becomes a strategic partner in Eastern Europe. Algo takes over 25 % of company shares and increases the shares in 2005 to 51%. Roman Gorovoy takes over the management.

By taking over 100% of the shares of ELECTROSTAR Schöttle GmbH Co. KG. the company will be renamed in 2008 to ELECTROSTAR GmbH.

Im Bereich der Elektrowerkzeug-Sauger werden mit der Einführung des ISP neue Maßstäbe auf dem Markt gesetzt - Filterabreinigung ohne Luftstromunterbrechung. 

Mit der patentierten Druckdifferenzmessung im ISP iPulse, bringt Electrostar 2013 eine bahnbrechende Innovation auf den Markt. 

With a more progressive market presence and more customer orientation, the foundation for the future is laid. The NSG uClean series replaces the previous GS and HS series and bring out a breeze of fresh air to the product portfolio of Starmix.
